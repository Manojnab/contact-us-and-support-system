What is RMM Pass?

Ola pass offers the convenience of hassle-free travel with its flat fare rides. Ola pass is available in two variants i.e. Cab Pass and Auto Pass.

You can choose to buy a cab or an auto pass as per your travel requirement. Please ensure that you have selected the correct city as each city has its unique set of cab/auto passes and will be applicable to the rides taken in that particular city and not in any other city.

Checking Total Fare

Alternately, you can also enter the drop location and choose the cab category to check the estimated Total Fare for your ride.

Note- The estimated total fare for your ride may vary due to traffic conditions or any variation in distance traveled.
