What are the car types available in Micro and Mini?

Micro and Mini are the most economical and popular cab category in RMM travels.

In Micro and Mini category, cars like Maruti Ritz, Nissan Micra, Tata Indica, Maruti WagonR, Hyundai Eon, and Datsun Go are available.

How much luggage can I carry in my Micro or Mini ride?

Micro and Mini rides can accommodate up to 2-3 small size bags.

What is the seating capacity for Micro and Mini?

Both Micro and Mini can seat upto 4 passengers.

Note- Children above 5 years will be considered as single passenger.
