How can I check the fare for my RMM ride?

Select the Rate Card option available in the RMM app menu to check the fares for RMM rides in different cities.

Checking the Fare for a Cab Category

Depending upon the type of car u select (Ex: Mini, Sedan or Prime) and per km charges for the ride also varies upon the type of car.Depending upon these factor the fair is calculated.

Checking Total Fare

Alternately, you can also enter the drop location and choose the cab category to check the estimated Total Fare for your ride.

Note- The estimated total fare for your ride may vary due to traffic conditions or any variation in distance traveled.
